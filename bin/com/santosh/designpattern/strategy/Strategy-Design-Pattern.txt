Strategy Design Pattern
	- It allows us to define a family of business rules/algorithms, encapsulate each one and make interchangeable at runtime.
	
	- Example
		Claim Settlement System - claim will be settled depending on the type of claim.
			1. Cashless claim 
			2. Reimbursement claim   
			
	- Advantage
		1. Plug-and-play rules: Easily add new settlement strategies (e.g., corporate insurance, co-pay models).
		2. Scalability: Different insurers or products can reuse the same framework with their own strategies.
